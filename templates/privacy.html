Privacy Policy

This privacy policy provides how we (GPSClean Web) use and protects any information that you give or share when you use this website.
We may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page from time to time to ensure that you are happy with any changes.
This policy has been effective since 29.12.2023 updated 29.12.2023.

Which data we collect

The only data that is collected, and in a temporary way, is the GPS trace (in the .gpx format) that you explicitly upload on the website to test the correcting model.

For how long do we keep your data?

The GPS trace is kept in the system just for the time needed to process it and is deleted immediately after. Thus, after a few seconds, the trace is permanently deleted from the system and no persistent storage is used or involved in the process.

Which cookies do we use?

Well, none at all. No cookie or analytical tracker is used in this website.

I have a question, who should I contact?

For any question, please feel free to contact us on GitHub or via email.
